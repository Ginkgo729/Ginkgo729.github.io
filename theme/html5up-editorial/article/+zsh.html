ZSH 安装设置

CentOS

安装完成后退出重新登录终端即可

```sh yum install -y zsh chsh -s /bin/zsh curl -L https://raw.github.com/robbyrussell/oh-my-zsh/master/tools/install.sh | sh

or

sh -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.github.com/robbyrussell/oh-my-zsh/master/tools/install.sh)"

or 自行建立脚本运行

https://github.com/ohmyzsh/ohmyzsh/blob/master/tools/install.sh
